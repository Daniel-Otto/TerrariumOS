# Terrarium Equipment

Essential equipment for climate control and habitat maintenance.

---

## Climate Control

### Rain System

| Item | Description | Price (approx.) | Notes |
|------|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Exo Terra Monsoon Multi | Programmable rain system | 80€ | Recommended |
| Exo Terra Monsoon Solo | Single nozzle version | 60€ | Smaller setups |

**Features:**
- Programmable misting system
- Multiple nozzles (Multi version)
- Adjustable spray duration
- External water reservoir

**TerrariumOS Integration:**
- Controlled via 230V relay (ON/OFF)
- Activates only during time windows + humidity threshold
- No fixed timer – responds to actual humidity levels

**Alternative:**
- MistKing system (more expensive, professional-grade)
- DIY fogger + pump setup

## Maintenance Schedule

| Task | Frequency | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Refill water reservoir | Weekly | For rain system |
| Clean nozzles | Monthly | Prevent clogging |
| Replace substrate | 6-12 months | Depends on use |
| Prune plants | As needed | Keep growth in check |
| Check drainage | Monthly | Prevent overflow |

---

## Safety Notes

- Always use GFCI outlets near water sources
- Keep electronics away from direct spray
- Monitor humidity levels to prevent mold
- Ensure proper ventilation to avoid stagnant air
